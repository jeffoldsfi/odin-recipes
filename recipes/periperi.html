Peri peri chicken wings

This recipe will help you make delicious peri peri chicken wings for four people. It requires little ingredients and is one of the easiest recipes on our site. I n 30 minutes of prep. time, you'll be ready to enjoy these delicious wings.

Ingredients:

  • 8kg chicken wings
  • Kewpie mayo, to serve
  • Sliced green chilli, to serve
  • Peri peri sauce
